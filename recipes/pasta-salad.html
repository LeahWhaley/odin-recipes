Pasta Salad
Description

Pasta salad is, as the name implies, a dish that contains one or more types of pasta as well as other vegetables as preferred. It can be served either chilled or at room temperature but is never served hot. Can also be tossed in some sort of dressing if preferred.

This is usually served as an appetizer or first course of a meal. It is never typically the main course because it is very light. It is also typically served at cookouts, wedding receptions, family reunions, and other similar gatherings. It is relatively easy to make and only requires a few ingredients. In fact, the only required ingredient is the pasta although it is usually served with at least one other ingredient. It is highly customizable because anything can be added.

Ingredients
  • 1 (16 ounce) package fusilli (spiral) pasta
  • 3 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 pound provolone cheese, cubed
  • 1/2 pound salami, cubed
  • 1/4 pound sliced pepperoni, cut in half
  • 1 large green bell pepper, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 (10 ounce) can black olives, drained
  • 1 (4 ounce) jar pimentos, drained
  • 1 (8 ounce) bottle Italian salad dressing
Steps
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook fusilli pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain.
  3. Combine pasta with tomatoes, cheese, salami, pepperoni, green pepper, olives, and pimentos in a large bowl. Pour in salad dressing; toss to coat.
  4. Enjoy!
